About Our Charity Shop

Supporting the local hospice through community-driven initiatives.

Our Mission

Our charity shop aims to support the local hospice by raising funds through the sale of donated goods. Every purchase contributes to providing essential care to those in need.

What We Offer

Our stores are filled with unique items, including clothing, books, furniture, and more. Each item has been generously donated, offering you the chance to find something special while supporting a great cause.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have contributed over $500,000 to the hospice, funding critical care and services. Your support helps us continue making a difference in our community.
